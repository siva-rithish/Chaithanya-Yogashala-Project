About Us

Rama Krishna Prasad B

Welcome to Chaithanya Yogashala, where the ancient wisdom of yoga meets modern practice, inspired by the teachings of the legendary B.K.S. Iyengar. Nestled in the heart of Suchindram, our center is a sanctuary for those seeking balance, strength, and inner peace through the transformative power of yoga. At Chaithanya Yogashala, we honor the profound legacy of B.K.S. Iyengar, a visionary yogi whose teachings have touched countless lives worldwide. With a deep reverence for his dedication to precision, alignment, and mindfulness, we strive to uphold his legacy by offering authentic, accessible yoga experiences for practitioners of all levels.

Join us and take your yoga to the next level. Your journey starts here.
