Welcome to Chaithanya Yogashala, where the ancient wisdom of yoga meets modern practice, inspired by the teachings of the legendary B.K.S. Iyengar. At Chaithanya Yogashala, we honor the profound legacy of B.K.S. Iyengar, a visionary yogi whose teachings have touched countless lives worldwide. With a deep reverence for his dedication to precision, alignment, and mindfulness, we strive to uphold his legacy by offering authentic, accessible yoga experiences for practitioners of all levels.
